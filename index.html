Bio

I’m an economist working with Data Science and currently living in the beautiful city of Florianópolis, Brazil. I’m an advanced user of the R language, Shiny Framework and an avid learner in the Machine Learning subject. Some of my hobbies include Science Fiction stuff, History, Racing and Aviation (I love to fly, both in real life or simulation).

I have professional experience in the educational, financial and public sector, and also in a dedicated data science company, where we were responsible for creating incredible Artificial Intelligence based products and applications for big companies. I also have experience in the field of Risk and Compliance, being responsible for building and monitoring financial risk indicators in accordance with the regulator.

I currently work as a Data Scientist at PicPay, a large Brazilian Fintech company with more than 50 million customers. I am co-responsible for planning and implementing the Credit Models Monitoring Project and its respective products and tools.
